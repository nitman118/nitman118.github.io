About

Bridging research and the shopfloor

I recently completed my PhD in Industrial Engineering at TU/e, where I worked on dispatching and scheduling algorithms for autonomous fleets in high-mix, low-volume manufacturing.

Today I split my time between two roles: as a Data Scientist at KMWE I build digital platforms, AI agents, and analytics tooling for daily production; as a part-time Assistant Professor in the AIMM group at EAISI, I research intelligent and interoperable shopfloors — Unified Namespace architectures and agentic AI for adaptive production.
