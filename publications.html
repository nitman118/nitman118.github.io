A Digital Platform for Heterogeneous Fleet Management in Manufacturing Intralogistics

Handbook on Digital Business Ecosystems in Manufacturing · 2024
N. Singh, A. Akcay, Q.-V. Dang, I. Adan, E. A. Thijssen

A digital platform for serving multiple tenants of Brainport Industries Campus sharing a fleet of heterogeneous automated guided vehicles. The platform combines an order-input UI, an order database, and a central cloud server housing the dispatching intelligence.

Dispatching AGVs with Battery Constraints using Deep Reinforcement Learning

Computers & Industrial Engineering · 2024
N. Singh, A. Akcay, Q.-V. Dang, T. Martagan, I. Adan

Real-time dispatching of an AGV fleet with battery constraints, formulated as a Markov Decision Process and solved with deep reinforcement learning. AGVs must be assigned to randomly arriving transport requests under stochastic demand.

Simulation-based AGV Management with a Linear Dispatching Rule

Winter Simulation Conference (WSC) · 2023
N. Singh, J. B. H. C. Didden, A. Akcay, T. G. Martagan, I. J. B. F. Adan

Real-time dispatching of a heterogeneous AGV fleet with battery constraints. An AGV-specific linear dispatching rule is learned to assign vehicles to randomly arriving transport requests, minimising a weighted sum of tardiness and travel costs. Validated on real-world data from an industry partner.
